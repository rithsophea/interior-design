About Us

Our mission is to Make Life Beautiful for ourselves and those around us.

Interior Design Company is a multi-disciplinary interior design firm based in Salt Lake City, Utah, known for creating beautifully balanced spaces that are both classic and current. In addition to our portfolio of luxury design clients both domestic and international, Interior Design Company creates thoughtful, guide-focused design and lifestyle content for fans to replicate the Studio Interior Design Company aesthetic on their own. The Netflix-based television series Dream Home Makeover, two best-selling books, Make Life Beautiful and The Art of Home, as well as partnerships with preeminent brands including Target, Kohler, Ann Sacks, and MasterClass.
